The problem

Five tools that each forget what the others learned

The idea-to-launch journey is not missing tools. It is missing continuity. You validate in ChatGPT, run interviews with The Mom Test discipline (if you do them at all), build in Lovable, and hunt for GTM advice in Twitter threads. Every handoff loses the context the previous step produced.

Most tools also cheerlead. They confirm a market exists and call it validated. Real validation means a real person confirmed your solution fits inside their actual workflow — and most founders never get there because no tool forces them to.

ChatGPT cheerleads

Tells you your idea is great. Forgets it the next conversation. Never asks you to talk to a customer.

Reports stop at market size

Data services confirm a market exists. They cannot tell you whether your solution fits a specific workflow.

Builders start from scratch

Lovable and v0 do not know what your customer interviews revealed. The build ignores what validation found.

Every handoff resets context

Moving between tools means re-explaining your idea, your customer, and your findings from scratch every time.

How it works

Four stages. One memory.

Each stage feeds directly into the next. Nothing you learn is left behind.

Stage 1

Honest validation — no cheerleading

Submit your idea. Get a BUILD / KILL / PIVOT verdict backed by sourced reasoning. The verdict does not move until you log real customer conversations.

Stage 2

Customer conversation loop

Log conversations with structured prompts. Each entry visibly shifts the verdict. The platform extracts customer language, confirmed pain points, and workflow gaps — and stores them.

Stage 3

Validation-to-build handoff

What you learned drives the spec. We pass confirmed customer language, pain points, and workflow gaps directly into your AI builder prompt — so the first build starts informed, not from scratch.

Stage 4

Context-aware ongoing guidance

GTM strategy, pitch prep, and fundraising advice — all informed by your actual history. No re-explaining. The platform already knows your idea, your customers, and what you built.

Features

Built for founders who do the real work

Every feature is designed around the job that actually matters: confirming real workflow fit before writing a line of code.

Verdict that moves with evidence

The BUILD / KILL / PIVOT verdict is not a one-time score. It recalculates as you log conversations, showing you exactly which assumptions are confirmed and which are still unproven.

Structured conversation logging

Guided prompts keep your interviews on track — inspired by Mom Test discipline. The platform extracts signals, not summaries, so you do not fool yourself with polite answers.

Informed MVP spec generation

When you are ready to build, the platform generates a concise spec populated with your customer language and confirmed workflow gaps — ready to paste into any AI builder.

Persistent memory across every stage

Everything you enter — your idea, your customer quotes, your build decisions, your launch results — lives in one place. Stage 4 guidance knows what Stage 1 found. No re-briefing.

GTM, pitch, and raise guidance

Context-aware strategy advice for go-to-market, investor pitch, and fundraising — tailored to your specific product, your specific customers, and your specific stage.
