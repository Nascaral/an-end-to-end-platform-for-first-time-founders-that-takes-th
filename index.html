The real problem

It's not that you're using the wrong tools.

Each tool in your stack is genuinely good at its one thing. ChatGPT brainstorms. Data reports surface market size. Lovable builds UIs. The problem is the handoff — what you learn at each stage evaporates before the next tool can use it.

You end up rebuilding context from scratch every time. Your customer interviews don't inform your MVP brief. Your MVP brief doesn't inform your GTM copy. Every tool starts cold.

Validation tools that cheerlead

Most AI tools confirm whatever you bring them. They don't push back, they don't demand real conversations, and they'll call anything "validated."

The handoff is where context dies

Copy-pasting insights between tools isn't a workflow — it's a tax you pay every single step. The next tool never has the full picture.

Guidance that doesn't know your history

Blog posts and generic AI advice are written for someone else's startup. None of it knows what you've already learned or where you've already been.
